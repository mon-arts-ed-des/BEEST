Cards

The result of this process

The intention for this element is to contain important information and make it stand out like learning outcomes or lecturer contact details. When using Bootstrap cards, we have pre-coded them to format in a certain manner. One card will be centred on the page and half the width of the screen. Two or more cards will take up equal width on the screen, i.e. Two cards will be half the width of the screen and 3 cards a 1/3 of the width of the screen each.
