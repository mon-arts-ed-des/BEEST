Video

BEEST's Video element creator takes a video link from Panopto/YouTube/Vimeo/TED Talks along with some context about what is in the video, and combines it into a responsive format. This means as the device you are viewing the video on gets smaller, the information on the page resizes to fit the device, until eventually the context and video collapse underneath one another to form one column on the page.
