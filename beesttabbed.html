Vertical Tabs

The result of this process

This process will generate a set of vertical tabs on the left that alternate content displayed on the right when clicked. Tabbed content can be easily misused.

  • Tabs should be used to contain information such as frequently asked questions (FAQs), or putting together important unit information such a Synopsis, Learning Outcomes, Assessment Summary all in the one place. Or as a recap of the important aspects of the content from the lesson/week/topic/module.
  • If they get used on every single page they end up frustrating users who constantly have to click to reveal information.
  • if you put too much information in the content on the right, the user will have to scroll which defeats the purpose of keeping information concise.
